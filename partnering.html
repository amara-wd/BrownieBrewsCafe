TRAINING AND SUPPORT

People from all walks of life can become successful Brownie Brews Coffees franchise partners. No prior coffee knowledge is required, although you need a passion for all things coffee! We will arm you with the tools you need to set up and run your coffee house.

ONGOING SUPPORT AND TRAINING

When you join our franchise network, we are committed to supporting your success, offering assistance in areas such as store management and staff training. Being part of a franchised system also means you have access to tried and tested operating systems, designed to help your coffee house grow from strength to strength.

MARKETING

Brand identity has always been central to the success of Brownie Brew's Coffees. Our global marketing dep-artment has years of experience in creating and implementing successful marketing campaigns to help drive guests into our coffee houses. In Pakistan, Brownie Brew's Coffees offers franchise partners ongoing advice on local-area marketing strategies and provides access to up-to-date brand assets. We also invest in targeted campaigns aimed at boosting consumer engagement through social and print media and through radio advertising.

REQUIRED INVESTMENT & APPLICATION PROCEDURE

The investment required to start up a Brownie Brew's Coffee franchise in Pakistan is around Rs. 30 million. This includes a non-refundable store opening fee, equipment, build up, materials and other set up costs. Once established, ongoing royalties are charged at 8.5% of sales. We encourage prospective franchise partners to contact us for a preliminary discussion. After this, should they wish to proceed further, they will be asked to submit a formal proposal in writing, along with other relevant documentation, such as their personal and financial history, site address and photos, and a complete business and feasibility plan. The application review process takes up to 6 weeks. Establishing a new Brownie Brew's Coffees franchise in Pakistan typically takes 6 months, from the date an application is submitted.
